EB-2/NIW Business Plan

Candidates need an advanced degree and progressive work experience in a relevant field. Applicants must also demonstrate a good ability in arts, business, and science. After this, applicants need to show that their proposed endeavor has national importance.

People applying for NIW as an entrepreneur or EB-2 business visa need a comprehensive business plan with the application. A thorough business plan EB-2 NIW is important if your proposed endeavor is a business.

In short, if you're a startup, you need an EB-2/NIW Business Plan to strengthen your application.
